What is this?

I have stumpled upon several data visualizations that chronicle the obesity epidemic of our friends south of the border. I noticed that the states that were usually coloured the most violent shades of red (red being bad, of course) were states that we typically think of as...red. I took the number of states won by the GOP in the last three elections and used that number to classify the states as either obese or non-obese (I know, very scientific) and then compared this to the election results.

This was done purely out of interest and to play around with D3.js. I am not trying to express a political opinion or cause offense.
